#SuperEureka

Introduction

SuperEureka is a 2D underwater action platformer developed in Unity.

The player controls Eureka, a hero trained to defeat a powerful sea monster that is causing floods and destroying a peaceful village. The game combines platforming, obstacle navigation, and a final boss fight.

This game also features Hack Club flags placed within the world as part of the environment.

Controls

Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
Jump: Spacebar
Attack: K

How to Win

Enter the underwater world.

Complete the obstacle course filled with hazards.

Avoid spikes and cannon attacks.

Use trampolines and moving rocks to navigate difficult areas.

Reach the monster’s dungeon.

Move close to the Sea Monster.

Press K five times to defeat it.

Defeat the monster to save the village and complete the game.

Story

Deep beneath the ocean lives a dangerous sea monster that causes massive floods and destruction to the nearby village.

To stop the chaos, the villagers train a brave warrior named Eureka. He dives into the water and must complete a dangerous course filled with spikes, cannons, trampolines, and moving rocks. After surviving the obstacles, he reaches the dungeon of the monster where the final battle takes place.

Features

Underwater 2D platformer gameplay
Obstacle course with spikes and cannons
Moving rocks and trampolines
Final boss fight
Five lives system
Hack Club flags placed within the game world

Side Quest

Four stars are scattered across the map in hidden or difficult locations.

Collecting all four stars is optional but adds an additional challenge for players who want to fully explore the game. A chime sound plays whenever a star is collected.

Audio and Sound Design

The game includes original background music created specifically for SuperEureka.

Sound effects include footsteps, jump sound, star collection chime, boss fight music, death sound, and victory sound. These elements enhance immersion and improve the overall gameplay experience.

Lives System

The player starts with five lives.
If all lives are lost, the game ends.
